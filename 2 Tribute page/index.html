Musician and actor Jon Bon Jovi

Here's a time-line Jon Bon Jovi's' life:


  • 1980 Jon recorded his first single, Runaway, at his cousin's studio, with back-up by studio musicians. A local radio station included the song on a compilation tape, and it began to get frequent airplay. The success of Runaway got Jon noticed, and he realized that he'd need more than session players if he wanted to capitalize on his success.
  • 1984 The group's self-titled debut came out on January 21, 1984. Filled with their now-signature power ballads underscored by soaring guitar riffs and well-crafted melodies, the album went gold.   • 2017 In late 2017, marking an impressive journey that produced an estimated 130 million records sold, Bon Jovi was elected to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
